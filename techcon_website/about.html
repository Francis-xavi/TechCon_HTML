History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most influential tech conferences globally, drawing speakers and attendees from across the world.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and inspiration in the tech community. TechCon serves as a platform for professionals, students, and thought leaders to share ideas and shape the future of technology.

Past Speakers

Dr. Jane Martinez

Renowned AI researcher and pioneer in ethical machine learning. She spoke about the future of AI and the importance of transparent algorithms.
